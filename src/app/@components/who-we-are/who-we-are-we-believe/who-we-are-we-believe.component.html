We believe in changing the way Tourism and Hospitality is run in Africa. Our Ambition has been to foster equal opportunities for all women of African descent operating in the tourism & hospitality Industry. Our aim is to empower, promote and develop women to occupy key roles. That last part is key.

We believe in building capacity and delivering value for the industry through women who play an active role in the boardroom and frontlines. Where women have equality at the top, we see stronger, more resilient societies. They are natural care givers, they set the trends,they shape the industry.

We have committed to empowering our own African Women to grow, to deliver impactful, engaging and transformational work through best practices for the industry we are passionate about.

Our value drivers include training / knowledge transfer, instant mentoring, upskilling and networking to create a truly personalized journey to the top.

We are an association designed to empower women of African descent fulfil their economic and individual potential.

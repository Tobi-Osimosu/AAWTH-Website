Why we exist

To empower, promote and develop women to occupy key roles in the Tourism & Hospitality industry in Africa.

1

Empower women

We have committed to empowering our own African Women to grow, to deliver impactful, engaging and transformational work through best practices for the industry we are passionate about.

2

Promote women

Our value drivers include training / knowledge transfer, instant mentoring, upskilling and networking to create a truly personalized journey to the top.

3

Develop women to occupy key roles

We believe in building capacity and delivering value for the industry through women who play an active role in the boardroom and frontlines. Where women have equality at the top, we see stronger, more resilient societies.
